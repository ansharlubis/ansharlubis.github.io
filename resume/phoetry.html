PHO/ETRY: A Slice of Life is a collaborative project by Luthfan Anshar Lubis (@ansharlubis) and Nazalea Kusuma (@thenazaleakusuma) that combines photography and poetry to tell a story of a mundane life. There is no overarching theme in this, just a slice in the life of new adults and less-than-new adults wherever you are. This long-distance collaboration served as our ways to stay grounded and was completed in the midst of our respective regular busy, mundane lives in Tokyo and Bandarlampung.

PHO/ETRY: A Slice of Life gives you reprieve in its pages. Oh, and all of this might just be about you.
